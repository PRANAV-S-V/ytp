It is a bash script for downloading youtube playlist with a single command..

Here is a step by step guide:

Step 1 : run the command - ./start.sh  
         in your terminal. and follow the installation procedures and instructions that get displayed in your terminal.

Step 2: ytp "name of the plalist" "url of the playlist"

        eg: ytp "FolderName" "https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=tpIctyqH29Q&list=PL8dPuuaLjXtNlU..........."

The playlist will be downloaded in your home directory.
